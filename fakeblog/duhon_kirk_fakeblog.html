Summoner Name: Infiriae

Champion of Choice: Sivir

During the 2010's Infiriae competed quite confidently in the League of Legends ranked scene. Making a big splash during the early seasons with Mordekaiser.

However, as of 2021 and the start of season 11, he is now using Sivir to dominate his opponents in the midlane.
